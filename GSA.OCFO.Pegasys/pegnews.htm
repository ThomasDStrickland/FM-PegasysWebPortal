Pegasys News and Announcements

The Financial Systems Service Desk will be available until midnight (EDT) on September 30 to assist with year-end closing actions.

PEGASYS PLANNED OUTAGE

Pegasys will be unavailable so that the Office of Financial and HR IT Services can complete various year-end closing tasks.

AFFECTED AREA

All Pegasys users and all Pegasys functions will be affected.

SCHEDULE
  • Wednesday, October 1 - midnight to 7 a.m. (EDT)
  • Friday, October 3 - 8:30 p.m. (EDT) through Saturday, October 4 - 7 a.m. (EDT)
  • Friday, October 10 - 7:30 p.m. (EDT) through Tuesday, October 14 - 7 a.m. (EDT)
USER ACTION REQUESTED

Pegasys users are advised to close all Pegasys sessions prior to the outage.

QUESTIONS?

If problems persist beyond the scheduled dates and times, users are advised to contact the Financial Systems Service Desk at 1-866-450-6588 or via email at OCFOServiceDesk@gsa.gov

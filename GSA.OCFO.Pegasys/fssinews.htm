GSA adopts FSSI OS2 BPA for all Office Supply Purchases

  • On October 26, 2010, GSA began transitioning to the Federal Strategic Sourcing Initiative (FSSI) Office Supplies 2 (OS2) Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) for all office supply purchases. This Federal Acquisition Service BPA represents a combined effort by several Federal agencies to achieve cost-savings through collective buying power. OS2 BPA vendors collectively offer a complete office supply catalog, including toner and printer supplies, to meet all your office needs. The BPA will result in significant savings across the Federal government on office supply procurement and will help make the entire process simpler and more efficient. All future orders for office supplies must be made through one of the fifteen (15) vendors on the OS2 BPA using a GSA purchase card. Orders may be placed through GSA Advantage!, phone, email, fax, vendor websites or at retail locations and the FSSI OS2 discount will automatically apply if a GSA purchase card is used.

    Further questions may be directed to Hassan Harris (212) 264-4784 or Hassan.harris@gsa.gov

Pegasys Support >> Help

We’re here to help you!

There are many support options available to you.
First, your Functional Coordinator is located right in your Region to provide expert assistance.
He or she knows your regional policy and is the first stop for signing up to use Pegasys.
Service Representatives are very knowledgeable about service-specific Pegasys information and policies.

The OCFO Service Desk is available 12 hours per business day to answer your questions. Just give us a call at 1-866-450-6588.

For the self-starter, we have comprehensive User Guides and step-by-step Quick Reference Cards.
